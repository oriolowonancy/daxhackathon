Remote Participation

Great news! for those who will like to participate remotely for Happy Hack, remote participation is allowed.

Please kindly ensure you have selected remote participation during your application

Please follow the guidelines in submission and ensure you have a good and working internet connection during the time. You will also be able to tune into our livestream during the event.
Finally you must try to prepare a video of your team and solution in time

  • Your video must have a duration of 2 minutes maximum.
  • Your video must include a link to the source code in the description.
  • Introduce you and the team (You can also add your Group/company name)
  • Introduce the hack / showcase name and a brief description
  • Mention any other information you think is relevant.
  • Upload the Video on Youtube, Vimeo etc and send the link to hackaday@sci.ng
