Theme

Great new! for those who will like to participate remotely for DAXhack, You can send your video link.

Please kindly ensure you have applied on our website

Please follow the guidelines in making your video (Don't worry if you can't get a HD camera, use what you have provided the Audio-visuals are OK)

  • Your video must have a duration of 5 minutes maximum.
  • Introduce you and the team (You can also add your Group/company name)
  • Introduce the hack / showcase name and a brief description
  • List the resources used in your hack
  • Explain the hack, can be done through a simple presentation / demo / a little acting, feel free to be creative.
  • Please Label Video with Group/company name with "DAXhack" at the ending
  • Include Label of Hack / Showcase name
  • Upload the Video on Youtube, Vimeo etc and send the link.

We will like to promote and talk about your hacks, please feel free to send us further info on hackathon@disruptiveafricaexpo.com

  • Introduce the team and their backgrounds, as well as the company if the entry is from a company;
  • Describe the hack, a deeper dive into how you created the hack and its functionality, for example the libraries used and some of the problems you faced;
  • Share any code / links / demos that are available; and
  • Any other information you think relevant.
