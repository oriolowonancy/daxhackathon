Disruptive Africa Expo Hackathon 2015

Idea Nigeria Lagos Centre, 296 Herbert Macaulay Way, Sabo, Yaba

9 - 10 October, 2015

24 hours full time Disrupt for Good

Theme

Application of new/disruptive technology in crucial sectors of the Nigeria Economy including Telecommunications, Clean energy, Manufacturing, Agriculture, Health, Public Infrastructures and Education

Goals

The goal of DaxHack15 is to develop a rich and collaborative environment where developers can apply their skills in solving big problems using technologies like WebRTC, IoT, Telecom APIs, Data Mining, Blockchain encryption, and cloud Technologies. Successful applicants will be provided with resources and mentorship to brainstorm on/develop concept solutions

Benefits

All winning teams will be inaugrated as part of the pioneers of the Disruptive Africa Hackathon Community/Ninja Devs. As DAX Ninjas, members will gain access to exclusive resources and will have the opportunity to continue working with top firms in creating modern solutions.
